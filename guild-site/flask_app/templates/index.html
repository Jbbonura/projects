Welcome To Edema Ruh

What is the Edema Ruh? -A traveling troupe of performers, trading entertainment for food and coin, can be compared to the Romani. - We work hard to help run events and fill in holes. - We are a group of friends who enjoy fairs together! What Do We Do? -We perform Act, Sing, dance, Set up, Teardown, Cry, Run events, and kick ass. -We are volunteers that help make the magic of fair happen. -Black Powder, Swords fighting, Arts and Crafts, Opportunities to lead. -It's hard work, but we do it as a team, and after it might be your favorite memory.
